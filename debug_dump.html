Job Description

Job Overview

Job Summary

We are looking for a results-driven Senior Digital Marketing professional with strong experience in the Real Estate industry to lead and execute digital marketing strategies that generate high-quality leads, increase brand awareness, and support sales targets.

The ideal candidate will have hands-on experience managing property marketing campaigns, lead generation funnels, and performance marketing across multiple digital channels.

Key Responsibilities

1. Digital Strategy & Planning

  • Develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing strategies aligned with business and sales objectives.
  • Plan digital campaigns for property launches, project announcements, and seasonal campaigns.
  • Identify target audience segments (Investors – End Users – International Buyers).
  • Manage digital marketing budgets and optimize cost efficiency.

2. Lead Generation & Performance Marketing

  • Manage and optimize paid campaigns across:
  • Google Ads (Search – Display – YouTube)
  • Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)
  • LinkedIn Ads (if targeting investors or B2B real estate partnerships)
  • Build and optimize lead funnels and landing pages.
  • Monitor CPL (Cost Per Lead), CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), and ROI.
  • Implement retargeting and remarketing strategies.

3. Real Estate Campaign Management

  • Plan and manage campaigns for:
  • New project launches
  • Open house events
  • Property exhibitions
  • Broker & channel partner campaigns
  • Coordinate marketing activities with sales teams and brokers.
  • Support offline campaigns with digital integration.

4. Content & Brand Management

  • Supervise content strategy including:
  • Property videos
  • Social media content
  • Email marketing campaigns
  • Blog & SEO content
  • Ensure brand consistency across all digital platforms.

5. Data Analysis & Reporting

  • Track campaign performance using tools such as:
  • Google Analytics
  • Google Tag Manager
  • CRM Systems
  • Meta Business Manager
  • Prepare weekly and monthly performance reports.
  • Provide insights and recommendations for campaign optimization.

6. Team & Stakeholder Collaboration

  • Work closely with:
  • Sales Team
  • Creative & Design Team
  • External agencies (if applicable)
  • Mentor and support junior marketing team members.


Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business Administration, Communications, or a related field.
  • Minimum 5+ years of experience in Digital Marketing.
  • Minimum 2–3 years of experience in Real Estate Digital Marketing is mandatory.
  • Proven track record in generating qualified leads through digital channels.
  • Strong experience managing paid media campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads, etc.).
  • Experience working with CRM systems (Real Estate CRM experience is a strong plus).
  • Strong understanding of the Real Estate customer journey and property marketing lifecycle.
  • Experience managing digital campaigns for property launches and real estate projects.
  • Strong knowledge of analytics and reporting tools (Google Analytics, Tag Manager, Data Studio, etc.).
  • Experience in managing digital marketing budgets and optimizing ROI.
  • Excellent English communication skills (written and verbal).


Benefits & Perks


• Social & medical insurance

• Location: Concord Plaza – Fifth Settlement

• Working hours: 10 AM – 6 PM

• 2 days off
